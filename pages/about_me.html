Bio


I am a 3rd year PhD student in computer science at the doctoral school ED MSTII of Université Grenoble Alpes on the topic "Analysis, Optimisation and Debugging of BPMN Processes". I am working in the CONVECS team, a research team shared between INRIA Grenoble and LIG working on formal modeling and verification of asynchronous concurrent systems.
